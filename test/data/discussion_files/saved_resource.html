    I understand that there are an inordinate number of life insurance agents near military bases, and in some case, only certain sales people are permitted on base. They sell something called whole life, or universal life which are both extremely expensive and provide very little benefit. I think it's outrageous that any base commander would permit his/her troops to be sucked into this outrageous product. What they should be encouraging, if any insurance is encouraged, is term life insurance where you will always get the biggest bang for your buck. It's kind of like the base commanders are congress members who also don't represent us.
